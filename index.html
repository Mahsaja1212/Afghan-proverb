Afghan proverbs are short, wise sayings passed down through generations, offering insights into life, culture, and tradition.

.صبوری تلخ است اما میوه شیرین دارد

Patience is bitter , but its fruit is sweet.

I waiting or hardship is difficult, but the results are rewarding.

.هر که نان از عمل خویش خورد، منت حاتم طایی نبرد

He who earns his own bread owes nothing to the generous.

if you work for what you have, you don’t depend on anyone’s help.

.از قطره قطره باران سیل خیزد

From drops of rain, a flood rises.

Small actions lead to big results.

.هر چه از دل برآید،لاجرم بر دل نشیند.

What comes from heart, sets on the heart.

Sincere words touch deeply.

.سنگ بزرگ نشانه نزدن است.

A big stone means no intent to throw.

Big talk, no action.

.با یک گل بهار نمی شود

One flower does not make spring.

One sign is not enough for success.
